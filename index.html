Hi, I'm Frisbee.

I love making games that mix brains and heart (and sometimes ducks). Games that wake up curiosity and invite you to be present. I like when they’re short, too. Little, thoughtful things that fit into life rather than compete with it. I want them to feel human, like someone made them by hand, with care and a bit of soul showing through.

Behind the scenes, I craft them in Unity, hand-roll engines and tools in C++, and teach college computer science. To me, they’re all the same craft: understanding how things work, shaping them, and helping others do the same.

"Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once we grow up." — Picasso
