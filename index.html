About me

Hi, I'm Luke Frisbee! I love taking an idea (like a duck detective solving puzzles) and turning it into something people can actually play, explore, and smile about.

It’s always fun figuring things out with other people, whether it’s untangling a tricky mechanic or seeing how someone else’s perspective can take an idea in a direction you’d never expect. The best projects seem to come together when everyone feels heard, and the end result feels like something you couldn’t have made on your own. There’s nothing better than knowing what you’ve built makes sense to others, whether they’re playing it or helping bring it to life.
