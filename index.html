Bio

A small introduction about my self

Luke Frisbee

Game Developer & Computer Science Student

Hello! While I am not studying or tutoring Computer Science at Concordia University I like to spend my time working on personal software projects. I have a huge passion for not only developing video games but programming pretty much anything in general! Unity Engine, Visual Studio, and C# are my go-to tools for working on projects, although I love working with many others such as C++, Java, and Eclipse. Part of the reason why I enjoy programming and game development so much has to do with my love for problem-solving itself, I’ve always hovered towards hobbies that require critical thinking. Whether it’s been strategy-oriented games or having to analyze the patterns of an opposing football team. Whatever challenge or game I come across in life, growth is always my focus!

Projects

3D RogueLike
